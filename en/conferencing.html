Our highlights in an overview

Dial-Out Conferences

Dial-Out Conferences

In dial-out conferences, participants are called by the conference system. It is the most convenient form of conferencing and also the one with the highest participant and hold rate. Dial-out conferences can be combined with dial-in options..

Dial-In Conferences

Dial-In Conferences

In dial-in conferences, participants dial into the conference by themselves. After typing in a PIN, they enter the conference room. Landline and service numbers in various countries are available for dialling in.

Video Conferences

Video Conferences

Sometimes it is helpful to not only hear your business partners, but also be able to see them. In a video conference, the images and sounds of all participants are transmitted in sync.

Webcasts

Webcasts

A webcast is a broadcast or stream of an audio/video presentation via the World Wide Web. Webcasts are available as live webcasts and as on-demand webcasts (i.e. recorded versions).

Live Conferences

Live Conferences

A live conference is comparable to a real meeting. All participants are interactively involved and experience the event much more intensively. Live conferences support several moderators and up to 5,000 participants.

Playbacks

Playbacks

If an event is repeated or guest speakers are unable to attend, previously recorded sessions or individual contributions can be played back. Playback events can be interrupted and continued live.

Recordings

Recordings

Events can be recorded and are available to download as MP3 audio or MP4 video (full HD) after the end of the conference.

Requests to speak

Requests to speak

Participants can ask to speak via the telephone (*1) or via chat and be enabled by the moderators.

Registration forms

Registration forms

For an easy and quick registration of participants, web-based registration forms can be designed according to your own ideas.

Event Calender

Event Calender

All dates are displayed in easy-to-read event cards or in tabular form in an event calendar.

Branding Options

Branding Options

A branding tool is available for registration and survey forms. This can be used to promote events in your own corporate design with logos, banners, colours and photos of the speakers to suit the target group.

Landing pages

Landing pages

For the participants, individual login pages, also called landing pages, can be designed in the client's corporate design.

Dashboard

Dashboard

A clear cockpit displays all important key figures and events in the customer account and enables quick access to the most important areas.

Address book

Address book

A convenient address book is available for easy conference planning. Import, export and group functions allow events to be started in a blink of an eye.

Conference reports

Conference reports

After the end of the conference, in-depth reports and details of the events are available. They can be analysed online and downloaded in various formats.

Real-time control

Real-time control

For the control of the conferences, no plug-ins or software downloads are required. The high-performance HTML5 realtime console can be operated via any browser.
