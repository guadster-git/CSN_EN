From a trend to a reality not only in the finance and insurance industry

For quite some time now, the concept of counselling in the service sector has been changing. The classic consultation in the local office is being replaced by digital consultation via the internet. This change is not only taking place in the world of finance and insurance, but in all consulting-intensive sectors. For many people, online counselling is already as common as online banking.

The advantages of digital
counselling are obvious

Client and counsellor are no longer required to meet in person, saving time and travel costs. In one place and with a device of your choice, they can log into the online counselling session and can communicate with each other.

Our highlights

In a CSN online counselling session, the counsellor has a wide range of features at their hand to provide the client with an engaging experience and bring the counselling session to a successful conclusion.

Video chat

Video chat

At the beginning of a conversation, it is often helpful to establish a personal connection with your customer. Simply show your live video and ask your customer if they would like to transmit their video as well. In a video chat, it almost seems as if both parties are sitting in the same room.

Broadcasts

Broadcasts

Do you have product or explanatory videos that you would like to show your customers during a consultation? Our broadcast function also supports the integration of videos that you have stored on YouTube or other video platforms.

Online presentations

Online presentations

After your introduction, you can start a presentation with PowerPoint slides, PDF documents or images. Interactive content and engaging presentations turn your meeting into an exciting experience.

Digital signature

Digital signature

Contracts can be filled out together and released for download. If it needs to be done faster, the customer can also sign digitally. For example, the company inSign: The signature is absolutely legally secure, DSGVO-compliant and TÜV-tested.

Screen Sharing

Screen Sharing

Share your screen if, for example, you want to show special programmes on your PC, calculate an offer or fill out a contract together.

Business card

Business card

If video transmission is not desired or not possible at the moment, you can alternatively show your business card with your photo and contact information.

Whiteboard

Whiteboard

A short sketch often says more than a thousand words. Use our whiteboard instead of your flipchart or writing pad. You have various pens, colours, highlighters and other tools at your hands.

Text chat

Text chat

A text chat is helpful in a counselling session when some written information is needed to be exchanged quickly. For example, you can ask your clients to enter their email addresses or contact details.

File release

File release

Would you like to send your customer information material, an offer or the previously filled out contract? No problem, simply release the documents for download.

Quick surveys

Quick surveys

Engage with your customers during your consultation. Quick surveys can be very helpful in a sales or counselling meeting.

Opinion surveys

Opinion surveys

In addition to quick surveys, you can also create more complex survey forms in your account. Display these at the end of a consultation or send the link by e-mail, e.g. to receive feedback.

Telepointer / remote

Telepointer / remote

We can't transmit your index finger. But our red telepointer/remote captures your customer's attention just as much. You can also draw or write something down with it.

CSN online consulting

Offers advantages for many industries

Insurance companies and brokers

In close cooperation with the insurance industry, CSN has developed an online counselling platform that allows you to serve your clients securely and MIFID-compliant, even from afar. From the initial contact to the conclusion of the contract to the support for your existing customers. You can even use CSN to settle a claim online together with your customers.

  • MIFID- and FinVermV-compliant records
  • electronic signature
  • video consultation
  • media library
  • advisory documentation
  • quick start function

Banks / Financial Institutions

A customer no longer needs to visit a local bank for advice on banking and financial products. Even complex financial products can be explained simply and clearly in a CSN online consultation. Just as secure and personal as in your local branch.

  • Encryption technologies with the highest security standards
  • Contract conclusion with legally secure digital signature
  • Consultation documentation
  • MiFID II and FinVermV-compliant taping
  • DSGVO-compliant
  • OPDV certified
  • Hosted and Made in Germany

Real estate agents / architects

When it comes to real estate, there is a high demand for consultation between clients, real estate agents, architects and builders. Many issues can be clarified quickly and efficiently digitally via the CSN Online Consultation.

  • Online advice and information on objects
  • "Virtual tour" through the real estate
  • Discussing photos, floor plans, site plans
  • Valuation, cost calculation, etc.

Retail / e-commerce

Online shops, but also traditional retailers, are relying more and more on virtual consultation. A non-binding initial consultation on certain articles, sizes, colours or materials. Questions and uncertainties on the customers' part can be clarified in advance in a CSN online consultation and the purchasing process can be brought to a conclusion.

  • Integration into the online shop
  • Branding in your CI/CD
  • Sharing an account with colleagues
  • Quick start functions

Healthcare

In the health sector the online consultation has established itself, too. Perfectly suited for initial medical appointments, follow-up treatment, check-ups, consultations on findings and therapy sessions. No long waiting times and no risk of infections in the waiting room.

  • digital "patient journeys" for the best patient experience
  • anonymous online counselling in the (psycho)social field
  • online support in care
  • coaching & supervision

Telecommunications / energy industry

The energy industry and the telecommunications sector are in a transitional phase. Due to the energy revolution and digitalisation, the complexity of providers and products is rising and the customers' orientation on the market is declining.

  • Advise customers individually in a 1-to-1 dialogue
  • Guide prospective customers through your offer
  • Build trust in a safe environment
  • Help prospective customers to conclude the contract

Operation & Security

The CSN online consultation platform is offered as "Software-as-a-Service", in short: SaaS. This concept is based on the principle that CSN´s software and IT infrastructure are centrally operated and can be used by the customer via the internet with every common browser.
